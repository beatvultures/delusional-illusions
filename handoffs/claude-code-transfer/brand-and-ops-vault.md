## Brand Brain
- **Vibe**: Local, creative, warm, direct, and a bit surreal.
- **Audience**: Teens and young adults in Missoula, MT.
- **Core Promise**: Professional-grade custom nail art, made locally in Missoula, without salon prices or appointment stress.
- **Negative Space**: NOT a luxury salon, NOT corporate, NOT an influencer, NOT a licensed medical/legal service.

## Offer Ladder
- **Ready-Made Set**: $25 (Fast, a bit of style)

- **Event Custom Set**: $60 (High-detail, high-stakes events)

- **Elite/Bridal**: Starts at $75

## Order System
- **Flow**: Request → Sizing → Quote → Deposit → Paint → Pickup.
- **Intake Fields**: Name, Phone, Event Date, Package, Design Idea, Colors, Sizing Status, Inspiration Link.
- **Payment**: Manual confirmation/deposit (Venmo/CashApp).
- **Pickup**: Public pickup in Missoula.
